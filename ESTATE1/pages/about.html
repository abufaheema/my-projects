GRANDEUR cribs LIMITED

Grandeur cribs Limited is a registered commercial real estate company that is into sales, buying, leasing/renting and management of commercial and private properties. The company is established in 2019 to guarantee a rest of mind in your quest for shelter and land.

Through a combined efforts of our professionals, we conduct a due diligence to ascertain the legitimacy of every property offered for sale and a thorough evaluation of every prospective tenant. We are committed to adding value to every property under our management.

With our headquarters in Port Harcourt, South South Nigeria, we are able to reach out to other parts of Nigeria where our team of professionals cover. We guarantee a rest of mind to all our clients through our core values.
